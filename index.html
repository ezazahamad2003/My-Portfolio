Mechatronics:

I also conducted part and assembly design using Solidworks, tolerance calculations, and GD&T, as well as manufacturing methods. In addition, I organized and led advanced manufacturing and robotics workshops, utilizing my skills in leadership, critical thinking, and teamwork.

My software skills include Blender and Solidworks, and I am also skilled in documenting the development process with detailed documentation and instructions for the operation and maintenance of the prototype.
